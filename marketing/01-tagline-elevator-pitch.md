## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

APRS-TUI is the first terminal-based APRS client for amateur radio. It runs over SSH, in tmux, on a headless Raspberry Pi, or anywhere you have a terminal. Connect to Direwolf over your network, pair a Bluetooth HT, plug in a Mobilinkd TNC4 and connect over BLE out of the box, or tap into APRS-IS, all through a guided setup wizard that handles the hard parts for you. On macOS with a DigiRig, included scripts set up Direwolf on demand so you're on the air in minutes. Decode packets, track stations, beacon your position, and chat with other operators in per-station threaded conversations that feel like text messaging, complete with delivery confirmation, auto-retry, and persistent history that survives restarts. No GUI, no mouse. And it's designed with accessibility in mind: WCAG AA contrast ratios, color-blind-safe text prefixes on every packet type, a high contrast mode, monochrome fallback, and screen reader compatibility. It's open source, it's Python, and it's ready to run.
